About us

Welcome to Repair Masters LLC! Specializing in drywall repair and painting, we are your go-to experts for all your home renovation needs. Our services also include framing and a variety of other projects to help transform your space.

Founded in 2023 by owner and manager Brian Koski, Repair Masters brings over 18 years of experience in the trades, with carpentry being Brian's main expertise. Our commitment to quality workmanship, attention to detail, and excellent customer service sets us apart from the rest.

As a family-owned business, we take pride in treating our customers like family, ensuring that every project is completed to your satisfaction. We understand the importance of staying within budget, which is why we offer competitive pricing without sacrificing quality.

Whether you're looking to repair a damaged wall, freshen up your paint, or embark on a full home renovation, Repair Masters LLC is here to help. Contact us today to schedule a consultation and let us turn your vision into a reality.
